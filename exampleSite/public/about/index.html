About Our Company

Epyphite team was born with a vision to transform our daily lives, safely with software. Our team primary focus is to provide the needed ecosystem to empower communities though technology. Born in Buenos Aires in 2007, epyphite has been present in major cities across the Americas, Europe and Asia, to finally establish itself in Singapore. Our Mission is to provide agile and actionable solutions to an ecosystem of people. regulators , corporate and innovators, aiming to become a better, safer, fair and resilient society.
